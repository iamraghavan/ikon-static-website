About Us

Discovering IKON Construction

IKON Construction is a leading architectural and construction firm based in Chennai, India. With over 3 years of hands-on experience, we are driven by the vision of our Founder & CEO, Mohamed Ishaq Syed, and the strategic guidance of our Managing Director, R. Karaunainathan.

Our commitment to excellence is seen in our innovative and unique designs, bold project execution, and solutions tailored to client needs. We specialize in both interior and exterior design, blending aesthetics with functionality. From residential to commercial, our approach focuses on quality craftsmanship and industry-best construction techniques.
